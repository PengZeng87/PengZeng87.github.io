Research
Photonic hooks from Janus microcylinders

Guoqiang Gu, Liyang Shao, Jun Song, Junle Qu, Kai Zheng, Xingliang Shen, Zeng Peng, Jie Hu, Xiaolong Chen, Ming Chen, and Qiang Wu

Optics Express (2019)
https://doi.org/10.1364/OE.27.037771

A new approach of utilizing the symmetry-broken of the microparticles in material composition for the generation of PHs from Janus microcylinders is presented and implemented.
Numerical investigation of photonic nanojets generated from D-shaped dielectric microfibers

Guoqiang Gu, Xingliang Shen, Zeng Peng, Xinfeng Yang, Sankhyabrata Bandyopadhyay, Jiansong Feng, Dongrui Xiao, Liyang Shao

SPIE/COS Photonics Asia, 2019, Hangzhou, China
https://doi.org/10.1117/12.2538025

A unique structure of D-shaped microfibers in terms of height, width of D-sector and curvature radius, which can be fabricated through optical fiber polishing and optical fiber tapering techniques, was presented and studied with a finite-element- based numerical method.
Photonic hook generated by twin-ellipse microcylinder

Xingliang Shen, Guoqiang Gu, Liyang Shao, Zeng Peng, Jie Hu, Sankhyabrata Bandyopadhyay, Yuhui Liu, Jiahao Jiang, Ming Chen

arXiv (2019)
https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.13146

A method to combine photonic hooks and twin photonic jets is presented and demonstrate. A single light plane wave and a symmetric microparticle, twin-ellipse microcylinder, are used in this research.
